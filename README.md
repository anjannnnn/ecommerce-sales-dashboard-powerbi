E-Commerce Sales Dashboard (Power BI)
A business-focused Power BI dashboard analyzing 25K+ e-commerce records to uncover trends, optimize performance, and drive data-driven decisions.

Includes Power BI dashboard (.pbix), dataset, and business insights
 
 Business Problem
-E-commerce businesses need visibility into sales performance, customer behavior, and profit trends to make informed decisions.

 Objective
-To analyze 25K+ sales records and identify key business insights using Power BI dashboards.

 Tools Used
- Power BI
- Python (Pandas)

 Key KPIs
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Quantity Sold
- Profit by Month

 Key Insights
- West region contributes the highest share of total revenue, indicating strong market demand
- Technology category generates the highest profit margins compared to other categories
- Sales show a noticeable dip during mid-year months (May–July), suggesting seasonal trends
- A small group of top customers contributes a significant portion of overall revenue
  
 Business Impact
- Enables stakeholders to identify high-performing regions and allocate resources effectively
- Helps track seasonal trends for better inventory and marketing planning
- Supports decision-making through real-time KPI monitoring**
 
 Business Recommendations
- Increase marketing efforts in high-performing regions to maximize revenue
- Improve strategy for low-performing categories to boost sales
- Optimize pricing and promotions during low-profit months
